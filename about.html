About Reach Screens

Built in Lloydminster.
For Lloydminster.

Reach Screens started with a simple idea: local businesses deserve an advertising option as modern as the national brands get — without the national brand price tag.

We install indoor digital screens inside local businesses across the city. Advertisers get high-frequency exposure in places where attention actually lives. Hosts get free screens, free advertising, and a better customer experience.

Everything is built, managed, and supported locally. No call centers. No corporate middlemen. Just a small team that cares about making local advertising work.

Our values

What we stand for.

Local first.

We're from here. We know the businesses, we know the streets — we build what the community actually needs.

Transparent pricing.

Why Reach Screens

What makes us different.

Hyper-local only.

We're not a national network with a Lloydminster footnote. We are Lloydminster. Every screen, every advertiser, every host — local.

In-house creative.
